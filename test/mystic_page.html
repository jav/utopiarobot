Combat Spells

You enter the Mystic Circle, the secret locale from which your wizards cast their spells.

From here, you may order your magical minions to cast their magic on the lands of your province and to use their powers against your enemies.

Some of these spells will take effect instantly, some will remain in place until triggered, then evaporate, and yet others will remain cast for a period of time, merely fading day by day.

Wizards have an amazing array of spells at their disposal, but can only cast so many at any time. Runes will be needed for your wizards to effectively do their magic.

You can learn more about these spells in the guide and visit the Mystics Council page for a report on what spells are currently cast upon your lands. To train wizards you must visit the Wizard Academy.
